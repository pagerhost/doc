Privacy Policy

Welcome to Alto PDF Scanner, which is provided by Alto Labs. We are committed to protecting the privacy of your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information Alto PDF Scanner collects, uses, and discloses about you when you access or use our application (collectively, “Our Services”).

Alto PDF Scanner is a fully offline application. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. All data remains on your device.

1. What data do we collect?

Alto PDF Scanner does not collect any personal data. The app operates entirely offline on your device.

To provide Our Services, the app requests the following device permissions:

Camera Permission. We request access to your device’s camera to allow you to scan documents and capture images for PDF creation. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Storage Permission. We request access to your device’s storage (including MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE on Android 11+) to allow you to browse, open, create, edit, and manage PDF files on your device. All file operations are performed locally. No files are uploaded or transmitted to any external server.

We do not collect:

  • Registration or account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Cookies or tracking data

 

2. How we use your data?

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to use beyond what is necessary for the app to function on your device.

All processing — including document scanning, image filtering, PDF editing, form filling, digital signatures, watermarking, and file conversion — is performed entirely on your device. No data is sent to any external server.

 

3. Security of Your Data

Your data security is important to us. Because Alto PDF Scanner operates entirely offline, your documents and files never leave your device through our app. The security of your data is protected by your device’s own security measures, including device encryption, screen lock, and app permissions.

We recommend that you keep your device’s operating system and security settings up to date to ensure the best protection for your files.

 

4. How We Store Your Personal Data

Alto PDF Scanner does not store any personal data on external servers. All documents, images, and files you create or edit using Our Services are stored locally on your device.

You have full control over your files. You may delete any files created by the app at any time using your device’s file manager or through the app itself. Uninstalling the app will remove all app-specific data from your device.

 

5. How We Share Your Personal Data

We do not share any personal data with third parties because we do not collect any personal data. No data is transmitted from the app to us or to any third party.

 

6. What Are Your Rights and Choices

Since Alto PDF Scanner does not collect personal data, traditional data subject rights (such as access, correction, deletion, and portability) do not apply in the conventional sense. However, you have full control over your experience:

Permissions. You can grant or revoke camera and storage permissions at any time through your device’s Settings > Apps > Alto PDF Scanner > Permissions.

Data Deletion. You can delete any files created by the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all app-specific data.

 

7. How We Process Children’s Data

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since our app does not collect any personal data from any user, no special provisions for children’s data are necessary.

 

8. Cross-border Data Transfers

Alto PDF Scanner does not transfer any data across borders. All data processing occurs locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any server, whether domestic or international.

 

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last Updated Date” at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

 

10. How to Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the use of your data, please contact us via email at altopdfscanner@gmail.com.
